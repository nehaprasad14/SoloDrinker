How to use This App:

  1. Create an account with us using our Facebook integrated system
  2. Return to the Home page and click "Check-In" when you're at the bar
  3. Afterwards you'll be able to see the locations of other nearby Checked-In users
  4. *not yet implemented* Click the dots on your map to make the user's profile appear
  5. For now use the table to select a drinking buddy
  6. *not yet implemented* Message them if you would like to meet up

Safety Tips

  • Only ever check in if you are in a public area
  • If you ever feel unsafe contact the bar staff
  • If your contact isn't who they said they were, leave and report this persons account
  • Check out when you no longer want your location to be broadcasted
